Activities

Tubing

Grab friends and family for an epic tubing sesh! Make the kind of memories that you'll treasure forever!

Getting here:

Look for parking in LOT C or close to the entrance of LOT C and follow the signs. Wear comfortable clothing and dress in layers. A water-resistant outer layer is highly recommended. Snow boots are best, but a good hiking shoe with traction tread will work. Wear gloves or mittens. Bring sunglasses - the snowy environment can be hard on the eyes even on a cloudy day. Sunscreen and a water bottle are both great ideas! Don't hesitate to ask our friendly staff for their recommendations Take breaks & have fun!

Skiing and Snowboarding

Alpine Ski Area:

White Pass is open daily (weather permitting) from 8:45 am until 4 pm. Check the snow report page for the most current information. Running daily through April 14th, running weekends only April 20/21 and 27/28

Nordic Center:

Open Thursday - Sunday and holidays from 8:30 am - 4 pm through April 7, 2019. Nordic is open daily during Christmas and New Year's holidays as well as the MLK and Presidents' Day Mondays. Visit the Nordic Center page for details.

Daycare

At White Pass, we make a special effort to create the perfect play environment for our guests, ages 2 - 6. Reservations required (before 1:30 p.m. on the day before) to guarantee admittance. Walk-ins are welcome if space is available.

RATES:

  • $65.00 per day (includes 2 snacks and lunch)
  • $45.00 1/2 day a.m. (includes 1 snack and lunch)
  • $40.00 1/2 day p,m. (includes 1 snack, add lunch for $5.00)
  • $17.00 hourly

Space is limited to 21 children and we offer a 7:1 student teacher ratio. Parents supply children with disposable diapers, a change of clothes and any special foods*. Rates are subject to change.
